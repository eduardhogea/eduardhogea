# Hi, I'm Eduard Hogea

![header](https://capsule-render.vercel.app/api?type=wave&height=260&section=header&text=AI%20%7C%20XAI%20%7C%20Neuro-Symbolic%20Learning&fontSize=34&fontAlignY=38)

I am a **PhD Student in AI/XAI** and an **ML Engineer at Magna Electronics**, based in Timisoara, Romania. My work sits at the intersection of **neuro-symbolic AI**, **explainable machine learning**, **computer vision**, **industrial anomaly detection**, **cybersecurity**, and **continual learning**.

I am especially interested in systems that do more than predict: models that expose useful explanations, support reasoning over learned representations, and remain practical enough to evaluate on real-world data.

## Research Focus

- **Neuro-symbolic AI and Logic Tensor Networks:** hybrid models that combine neural predictors with first-order logic, satisfiability objectives, and deductive reasoning.
- **Explainable AI for vision and time-series systems:** attribution fusion, SHAP-based analysis, feature importance, and human-aligned explanations for model behavior.
- **Cybersecurity and anomaly detection:** intrusion detection, security-alert monitoring, and automotive/industrial anomaly detection with interpretable ML pipelines.
- **Computer vision and video understanding:** Vision Transformer explainability, unsupervised segmentation from explanation maps, deepfake explainability, and soccer action spotting.
- **Continual and incremental learning:** exemplar-free class-incremental learning, catastrophic forgetting, feature translation, and optimization-based feature selection.
- **Applied ML and reproducibility:** data analysis, model evaluation, MLOps workflows, experiment tracking, model serving, and public research code.
