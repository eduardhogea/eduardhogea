# Hi, I'm Eduard Hogea

![header](https://capsule-render.vercel.app/api?type=wave&height=260&section=header&text=Big%20Data%20%7C%20AI%20%7C%20Neuro-Symbolic%20Learning&fontSize=34&fontAlignY=38)

I work at the intersection of **Big Data**, **Artificial Intelligence**, and **neuro-symbolic learning**, with a strong interest in applying research ideas to real-world security, monitoring, and computer vision problems.

## Focus Areas

- Neuro-symbolic AI and logic-guided learning
- Intrusion Detection Systems and security-alert monitoring
- Computer Vision and applied machine learning
- Data analysis, experimentation, and research prototyping

## Exchange Experience

- Studied at **Johannes Kepler University Linz** in Austria during my bachelor's degree.
- Completed a year at **Universitat Autonoma de Barcelona**, with a focus on Computer Vision.

## Conferences and Awards

- **SYNASC** - Presented a comparative analysis of deep learning and neuro-symbolic approaches, including the use of First Order Logic with deep learning backbones. The post-conference paper was published by IEEE.
- **Sesiunea de Comunicari Stiintifice Studentesti** - Received an award for work on practical neuro-symbolic models for cybersecurity and Intrusion Detection Systems.
- **Smart Diaspora** - Invited to present a poster on progress in neuro-symbolic AI research and related publications.
